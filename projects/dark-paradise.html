About the Film

Dark Paradise explores the complex relationship between ambition and morality in contemporary society. Set against the backdrop of Estonia's changing landscape, the film follows a young entrepreneur who must confront the consequences of their choices. Through intimate character studies and breathtaking cinematography, Dark Paradise questions what we're willing to sacrifice for success.
