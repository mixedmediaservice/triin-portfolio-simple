ABOUT

Triin Ruumet is an Estonian film director and producer with over a decade of experience in both narrative and commercial filmmaking. Known for her distinctive visual style and compelling storytelling, she has directed feature films, documentaries, and commercial campaigns for leading brands.

Her work explores themes of identity, ambition, and human connection, often set against the backdrop of contemporary Estonian society. With a background in both film production and visual arts, Triin brings a unique perspective to every project.
